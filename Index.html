My Bio

Ethan Anderson, also known as Hexsphere, is a 12-year-old Indy Game Developer and Ui Graphic Designer. He is an indie game developer making games in Scratch! He makes graphic art & Ui designs in Affinity Designer. He is a Behance and YouTube user. He is the winner of Remotion’s logo contest!
